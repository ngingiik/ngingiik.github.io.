Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and PayPal.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the United States. International shipping times may vary.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Yes, we offer returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in new, unworn condition with tags attached.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or on the shipping carrier's website.
